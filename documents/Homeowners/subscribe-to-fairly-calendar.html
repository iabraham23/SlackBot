Subscribe to Fairly Calendar

Sharing your Fairly reservation calendar with others via subscription link

Updated over 3 months ago
Table of contents

You can create a subscription link (ICS format) to your Fairly reservation calendar. This allows third parties, such as service providers, family members, or anyone else you choose, to view reservation dates in their own calendar apps (Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, Outlook, etc.).

The person subscribing does not need a Fairly account. They simply add the ICS link you provide to their preferred calendar app, and the reservations will appear as a separate calendar titled: Fairly: [Your Home‚Äôs Address] Reservations

This is a read only subscription. It does not give them access to your Fairly account, and they cannot edit your calendar. Events will be labeled as:

  • Owner reservation (when you‚Äôve booked time in your home yourself)

  • Guest reservation (when a guest is staying)

Guest names are never shared.

Important to know before sharing

  • Once you share an ICS link, it cannot be revoked.

  • It is up to you as the homeowner (or your collaborators) to manage who receives the link.

  • Anyone with the link will be able to view the reservation dates for your home.

Step 1: Copy your ICS calendar link

  1. Go to your home in Fairly.

  2. Navigate to the Calendar tab.

  3. Click Share icon (next to Today button), then click ‚ÄòCopy link‚Äô when prompted.

Step 2: Share the link

Send the copied link to whoever you would like to share it with. They can subscribe to the calendar in the app of their choice by following the instructions below.

Step 3: Subscribe to the calendar

Below are subscription instructions you can share for common calendar apps. The link to this article is public, so feel free to share it.

Google Calendar

Note: The Google Calendar mobile app does not let you add a new ICS subscription directly. You must add it from the web first.

  1. Open Google Calendar.

  2. On the left, next to Other calendars, click the + icon.

  3. Select From URL.

  4. Paste the Fairly ICS link.

  5. Click Add calendar.

  6. The calendar will now appear under ‚ÄúOther calendars.‚Äù

Apple Calendar

Apple Calendar on desktop

  1. Open the Calendar app.

  2. From the menu bar, click File > New Calendar Subscription.

  3. Paste the Fairly ICS link.

  4. Click Subscribe.

  5. Name the calendar (e.g., Fairly: 123 Main St Reservations).

  6. Choose Auto-refresh: Every day (or more frequent if desired).

  7. Click OK.

Apple Calendar mobile app

  1. Open the Calendar app.

  2. Select Calendars at the bottom of the screen.

  3. Scroll down, and select Add Calendar.

  4. Select Add Subscription Calendar.

  5. Paste the Fairly ICS link.

  6. Click Subscribe.

  7. Name the calendar (e.g., Fairly: 123 Main St Reservations).

  8. Toggle ‚ÄòEvent Alerts‚Äô preference as desired.

  9. Click Add.

Outlook

Note: The Outlook mobile app does not let you add a new ICS subscription directly. You must add it from the web first.

  1. Go to Outlook.com and sign in.

  2. Open Calendar.

  3. On the left, click Add calendar.

  4. Select Subscribe from web.
    ![Outlook web subscription screenshot]

  5. Paste the Fairly ICS link.

  6. Give the calendar a name (e.g., Fairly: 123 Main St Reservations) and color.

  7. Click Import.

What calendar subscribers will see

The shared calendar will only show reservation dates (when guests are in your home).

  • Names of guests or owner guests are not shared - they will simply show up as ‚ÄúGuest Reservation‚Äù or ‚ÄúOwner Reservation‚Äù.

  • Blocks are not included on calendar subscriptions.

  • Reservations are automatically added and removed from the calendar for anyone who has subscribed.
